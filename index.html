Meet Imani

Your guide to discovering beauty, purpose, and faith-filled living

Faith • Beauty • Growth

Hi, Beautiful — I'm Imani!

I'm so glad you're here.

I'm a faith-driven beauty influencer, digital branding coach, and the heart behind Glow with Imani — but before any of that, I'm just a woman who decided to trust God and bet on herself.

A few years ago, my life didn't look like this at all.

I went through a painful divorce that left me heartbroken and starting over as a single, full-time mom — with no plan, no blueprint, and no idea how I'd rebuild my life.

I didn't have a huge savings account. I didn't have connections. I didn't have fancy equipment or a big team.

But what I did have was a little bit of faith — and a whole lot of fire inside me to glow again.

From Rock Bottom to Radiant

I picked up my phone and started creating.

I shared my story, my healing, my faith, my beauty tips — and slowly but surely, women like you started to find me.

With consistency and God's grace, I grew my TikTok to 20,000 followers, my Instagram to 8,000, and my YouTube to almost 3,000 — all organically and with almost no money.

Today, I teach women how to do the same.

I believe your glow starts on the inside — with God, with healing, and with the courage to show up even when you're scared.

But it doesn't have to stop there — you can shine on the outside too, and build a brand that pays you, honors God, and empowers other women to grow.

Why I Do This

I created Glow with Imani for every woman who's ever felt like she lost herself — in heartbreak, motherhood, shame, or fear — and wondered if she could rise again.

You can.

You're worthy of a life that glows — spiritually, physically, financially.

What I Offer

Through Glow with Imani, you'll find:

Beauty:

Gorgeous wigs & products that help you feel confident and show up as your best self.

Faith:

Words of encouragement, prayers, and community to remind you you're never alone.

Growth:

Coaching, digital marketing tools, and step-by-step guidance to help you build your influence and income — right from your phone.

Let's Glow, Together

If you're ready to glow from the inside out — to heal, to grow, and to build something beautiful — I'm here for you.

Because if I can do it — single mom, no plan, no fancy studio — you can too.

So grab your coffee, open your Bible, and let's glow, beautiful.

With love & light,

Imani
